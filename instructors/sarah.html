About Sarah

Sarah Johnson is the visionary founder of Inner Light Studio, with over a decade of experience in the modeling and fashion industry. Her journey began as a professional runway model, working with top fashion houses and designers across the globe.

After years of perfecting her craft on international runways, Sarah discovered her true passion: empowering others to find their inner confidence and grace. She founded Inner Light Studio to share her expertise in posture, runway walking, and confidence building with aspiring models and individuals looking to enhance their presence.

Sarah's unique teaching approach combines technical precision with emotional intelligence, helping students not just walk better, but feel better about themselves. Her warm, encouraging manner has helped transform hundreds of students from all walks of life.
