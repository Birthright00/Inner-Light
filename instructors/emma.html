About Emma

Emma Rodriguez is a dynamic confidence coach and presentation specialist with over a decade of experience in transforming lives through communication and self-expression. Her background in psychology and theater arts creates a unique foundation for her teaching approach.

Before joining Inner Light Studio, Emma worked as a corporate trainer and motivational speaker, helping executives and professionals overcome presentation anxiety and develop commanding presence. Her work with stage performers and public speakers led her to discover her passion for helping individuals find their authentic voice.

Emma's empathetic and encouraging teaching style has helped hundreds of students break through limiting beliefs and discover their inner strength. She specializes in working with individuals who struggle with social anxiety, stage fright, and self-doubt, guiding them toward genuine confidence and self-acceptance.
