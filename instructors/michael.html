About Michael

Michael Chen brings a unique blend of dance, fitness, and modeling expertise to Inner Light Studio. With a background in professional dance and movement therapy, Michael specializes in helping students achieve perfect posture and fluid, graceful movement.

His journey began in contemporary dance, where he performed with renowned companies across Asia and North America. After transitioning into the modeling world, Michael discovered his passion for teaching body alignment and movement techniques that enhance both physical presence and confidence.

Michael's holistic approach combines elements of dance, Pilates, and mindfulness to create a comprehensive training experience. His students consistently praise his patient, detailed instruction and ability to break down complex movements into achievable steps.
